# ShopKart-E-Commerce-Analytics
Executive Summary ShopKart is a rapidly growing e-commerce company operating across major Indian cities.
The company sells products across categories such as:
Electronics
Fashion
Sports
Home Appliances
Lifestyle
As business volume increased, management started facing multiple operational and analytical challenges:
Declining customer retention
Increasing cancelled orders
Poor inventory planning
Uneven category performance
Revenue fluctuations
Low-performing products
The Business Intelligence team has been asked to analyze operational and sales data using MySQL and provide actionable business insights.
